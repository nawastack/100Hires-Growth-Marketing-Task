# Growth Architecture (Case-Study): 100Hires Growth Plan
Applying the research from my 10-expert repository to 100Hires.

### The Objective
To accelerate pipeline growth and establish 100Hires as the "AI-native" recruiting authority in the next 90 days.

### The 3-Step Growth Plan

1. **Phase 1: GTM Trust (The Saks/White Hybrid)**
   * **Action:** Instead of typical ad campaigns, deploy "Founder-Led" content that documents the engineering of 100Hires. Use Vlad White’s PR/SEO playbook to secure backlinks from recruiting-tech publications, positioning 100Hires as the technical authority, not just another tool.

2. **Phase 2: Conversion Architecture (The Nair/Ali Workflow)**
   * **Action:** Audit the 100Hires pricing and sign-up flow using Vivek Nair’s pricing-teardown logic. Simultaneously, use Amir Ali’s AI-prompting framework to rewrite our landing page copy, optimizing for a lower reading-level, which captures the "overwhelmed recruiter" persona better than jargon.

3. **Phase 3: The Behavioral Nudge (The Harhut Framework)**
   * **Action:** Apply Nancy Harhut’s psychological triggers to our email nurture sequences. Specifically, utilize "social proof" (using current customer metrics) and "loss aversion" (what recruiting teams lose by *not* using AI) to improve the reply rates on our dormant lead list.

### Success Metrics
* **Lead Quality:** Move from "Lead Volume" to "Demo Requests from ICP-fit companies."
* **Conversion Rate:** Aim for a 15% increase in pricing page-to-trial signups through better UX/copy.
* **Pipeline Velocity:** Reduce the time between "First Content Interaction" and "Sales Demo."
