# Transcript Summary: Building Digital Trust
**Expert:** Daniel Saks  
**Topic:** Executive GTM Strategy
**Source Link:** [Daniel Saks: Future of GTM Automation](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OqlE9uhnud8)

### Core Insights
* **Digital Trust:** In the AI-era, trust is the currency. Buyers are skeptical of AI-generated content. You must build "Digital Trust" through transparency, founder-led storytelling, and clear product utility.
* **Sales Velocity:** Velocity = (Number of Leads × Conversion Rate) / Sales Cycle Length. You can't just throw more leads at a broken sales cycle.
* **The "Un-marketing" Approach:** Instead of selling features, sell the outcome. The best way to build trust is to document the "engineering" of your business—show how you build, why you build it, and who it's for.

### Why this matters for 100Hires:
Saks’ framework of using founder-led storytelling to document the engineering process is the perfect antidote to AI-fatigue for 100Hires.
